Program Categories

We offer comprehensive educational programs for all age groups

Preschool Program (Ages 3-5)

Our preschool program provides a nurturing environment where young children develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play-based learning.

Program Highlights:

  • Play-based curriculum that fosters creativity and curiosity
  • Early literacy and numeracy foundations
  • Social-emotional learning and character development
  • Specialist classes in music, art, and physical education
  • Small class sizes with individualized attention

Elementary Program (Grades 1-5)

Our elementary program builds a strong academic foundation while nurturing each child's unique talents and interests.

Program Highlights:

  • Core curriculum in language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies
  • Project-based learning and hands-on activities
  • Foreign language instruction starting in Grade 1
  • Technology integration with 1:1 devices
  • Enrichment programs in arts, music, and athletics

Middle School Program (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program supports students through this critical developmental period with academic challenge and social-emotional support.

Program Highlights:

  • Departmentalized instruction with subject-area experts
  • STEM-focused curriculum with robotics and coding
  • Advisory program for academic and personal guidance
  • Extracurricular activities and competitive sports
  • Leadership development and community service

High School Program (Grades 9-12)

Our college-preparatory high school program equips students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences for success in higher education and beyond.

Program Highlights:

  • Advanced Placement and Honors courses
  • Comprehensive college counseling program
  • Career exploration and internship opportunities
  • Research and independent study options
  • Vibrant arts and athletic programs
